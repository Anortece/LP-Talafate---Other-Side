Other Side Technology™
Exclusive Innovation

What is
Other Side
Technology?

Other Side Technology allows operators to safely extend the aerial basket past obstacles — reaching the opposite side of structures, tree canopies, utility lines, and complex terrain. This proprietary articulation system dramatically improves productivity and reduces repositioning time in demanding agricultural and industrial environments.

  • Extended side access beyond obstacles
  • Greater obstacle clearance capability
  • Faster positioning and repositioning
  • Reduced operational downtime
  • Increased operator safety at all times
